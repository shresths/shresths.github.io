Creating Reuse Recipe Document

Detailed step-by-step procedure for creating and updating your reuse recipe document.

Your reuse recipe document is pre-created.

Your reuse recipe document is pre-created for you, which can be assesses in your dashboard under the unpublished tab.

  1. Click on Unpublished tab.
  2. Identify your manuscript by the Title/Manuscript ID.
  3. Click on Edit to start creating your reuse recipe document.
